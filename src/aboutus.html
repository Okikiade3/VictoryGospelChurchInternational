ABOUT US

Our Statement of Purpose

  • To preach the gospel of Jesus Christ, to open the eyes of people and turn them from darkness to light
  • To bring many people from all races of the world into the family of believers
  • To teach and deliver them from fear of satan to serve God without fear
  • To lad and teach them to demonstrate the new life as an evidence of conversion
  • To lead and teach them holiness
  • To assist them to discover and develop their God-given potentials and use same for the spreading of the Gospel
  • To make heaven after all.
